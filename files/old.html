Process updates

Changes about SSN & Power of Attorney(POA).

Changes about Legal papers about Death(POA).

  • ACH

    Set the ACH if the doc is signed. After setting up , Add two call activities, IFR-OBI and followed by AMC-ASC comment: Start Date: MM/DD/YY.

    If there is already an ACH setup to the account or failed to set it up due to incorrect routing or unsigned ACH, Properly notate the account, No need to upload the ACH to Filenet.

    There are documents called Direct deposit enrollment form (ACH-Deposit forms), Do not set these up. Check the comments and if necessary, forward the DDE to Supervisors_All.

  • Bankruptcy Documents

    Add call activity IFR-XBK, and forward the document to Bankruptcy_Specialists@westlakefinancial.com

  • BBB & FCRA letters

    Add call activity IFR-XOT, and forward the document to Westlake_CEC_Dept@westlakefinancial.com

  • Cease & Desist

    Add 2 call activities, IFR-OBI and then ACC-CED. Add DNC checkbox in Collection App too. If they wish to stop receiving letters from Westlake, add 3rd call activity ACC-SCL.

    If they wish to remove Cease & Desist, Add 2 call activities, IFR-OBI and then ACC-RCED. Do not forget to uncheck the DNC box in Collection App.

  • Credit documents

    If the file contains words such as "Authority to release Information, Credit Inquiry, Credit rating request," Add call activity IFR-XOT, and forward the document to CSR Request. Name it "Credit Inquiry."

    If the file contains word "goodwill", Add call activity IFR-OBI, and name it "Credit Repair."

    If the file contains words such as "dispute, validation, proof of debt, unathorized credit check", Add call activity IFR-OBI, and name it "Credit Validation".

    If the file is not related to any account, Save it in your folder and follow the file naming format: UTL - "Name" Credit dispute via fax MM.DD.YYYY. ie: UTL- John Doe Credit dispute via fax 02.01.2020

  • Dealer Compliance

    If the file contains words such as "Vehicle diagnostics, Dealer Fraud, complaints, Lemon vehicle" Add 2 call activities, IFR-OBI and DCR-DCR.

  • Death Certificate

    Add 2 call activities. IFR-OBI & ACC-DCP (If primary's DC) or ACC-DCS (If co-user's DC)

    DNC all good phone numbers in Collection App.

    Send an email to Agent OFRS if there is no interested party willing to retain the car, otherwise add Comment: "Third Party must payoff account or agree on a settlement within 30 days of contact, or Vehicle may be repossessed".

  • Extension Agreement

    Please validate if the form is signed and still not past the due date.

    If the EXT form is valid, Add call activity, IFR-SEA, Include the date when the form was signed in the comments.

    If the EXT form is invalid, Add call activity, IFR-OBI, add remarks as to why we treated it invalid and upload to Filenet.

  • FPD/FPC-related documents

    Add call activity IFR-XFP, and forward the document to FPDFollow-up

  • Garnishment documents

    Add call activity IFR-XOT, and forward the document to Consumer Litigation Specialists

  • Identification Cards (ID)

    Important: Do not upload these IDs to Filenet

    Some examples of IDs are Social Security, ITIN and Driver's license. Please save these IDs in your folder or if there is a known issue such as SSN Correction or Name Change, refer to SSN & Name updates .

  • Impound related documents

    Some documents such as Stored Vehicle notice & Notice of pending lien sale fall under Impound documents.

    Add call activity, IFR-XOT & forward the documents to Impound

  • Insurance

    Insurance documents such as Insurance IDs, Renewal, Declarations policy, Insurance notification and Policy Expiration/Cancellation should be forwarded to insurancedocuments .

    Insurance documents such as Claims, Loss, Settlement, Letter of Guarantee requests, ACV and legal documents(Police report,)related to car damages, Car theft or accident must be forwarded over to Insurance Claims .

  • Legal papers

    Court orders, Subpoena are some examples of Legal papers. Add 2 call activities, IFR-XOT and ACC-LRR; then forward it to Westlake Legal department .

    If the document is in relation to an ID Theft Claim, then add 3 Call Activities, IFR-XOT, ACC-LRR & DDE-IDT (from email), DDL-IDT(customer letter). Forward it to Westlake Legal department aferwards.

    If the legal doc is about death/ deceased member (Estate of the member), Please add IFR-XOT then forward it to Agent OFRS .

  • Military Orders related to SCRA

    Add call activity, IFR-XOT & forward the document to Westlake Compliance dept.

  • Notice Slip

    Add call activity IFR-OBI. In Collection App, update address,add new telephone numbers (if necessary), Put check on the Written consent box.

  • Payoff request

    Add call activity IFR-XOT, Forward the document to CSR Request.

  • Privacy opt-out

    Add 2 call activities IFR-OBI & ACC-POO

  • Payments

    Checks,Repair order, Refund, Overdraft, Double Charge, Mispayment, Payment research papers, will fall under this subject. Please check comments if there is an issue with their monthly payments. If there is, Tag IFR-XOT and forward the documents to Supervisors_All.

    If there is no issue, Add a tag IFR-OBI.

    Important: Do not upload Bank checks to Filenet.

    If the document is about the customer wanting to settle the balance soon, or making a promise to pay, Add tag IFR-XOT and forward the document to CSR Request.

  • Power of Attorney (POA)

    Add call activities IFR-XOT & forward the document to Agent OFRS.

  • Privacy opt-out

    Add 2 call activities IFR-OBI & ACC-POO.

  • Re-assignment of Contract

    Add call activity IFR-OBI

  • Returned Mail

    Add call activity RTM-NOI if the returned document was a Notice of Intent, otherwise RTM-RTM for all. Also include the date of return in the comments.

  • Reinstatement Documents

    Documents such as Third-party authorization to release/pick-up/retrieve vehicle, Stipulations (Stips), CHP Release form, 3 Character references, Proof of Employment, Water/Power Bill will be sent over to Reinstatement

    Add call activity IFR-XOT, Then forward to Reinstatement_Admins

    .

  • SSN Correction & Name updates

    If there is 1 digit of SSN correction, Follow these steps:

    1. Go to OFSLL.
    2. Go to Customer Service > Maintenance > Click +Add .
    3. Uncheck Monetary box, Type in "Customer maintenance" in Transaction.
    4. Load Parameters and fill out all the fields required (with check on it at the right side). Input the full SSN.
    5. IFR-OBI and notate the changes made.

    If there are 3 letters or less of Name correction, Follow these steps:

    1. Go to OFSLL.
    2. Go to Customer Service > Maintenance > Click +Add .
    3. Uncheck Monetary box and Type in "Customer name maintenance" in Transaction.
    4. Load Parameters and fill out all the fields required (with check on it at the right side). Input the correct first/last name.
    5. IFR-OBI and notate the changes made.

    If there are 2 or more digits of SSN Correction and Name change requiring us to change 4 letters, or changing the first/last name or the full name, Add call activity IFR-XOT and forward the document to WFS Customer Service .

  • Title documents

    For documents such as title release requests and etc, Please ensure that their account is already Paid off and Paid off for not more than 30 days. Add call activity IFR-XTT and forward the document to Westlake Titles .

    Documents from DMV (Dept. of Motor Vehicles) such as Certificate of Title, Registration of Vehicle & Registration plates will be processed differently. Add call activity IFR-OBI and add comment, "Please fax VR documents to 8778545682."

    For out-of-state lien requests or Title release request paid off for more than 30 days, Add call activity IFR-OBI and add comment, Please refer customer to "My Title support" for Out-of-state Title requests and Title Requests (Paid off past 30 days)".

  • Third-party Authorization

    Add call activity IFR-OBI and comment the authorized member, Do not forget to check the alert box .

  • Voluntary surrender

    Add call activity IFR-PVS and forward the document to Supervisors_All.

  • W-9

    Add call activity IFR-XOT and forward the document to CSR Request.
